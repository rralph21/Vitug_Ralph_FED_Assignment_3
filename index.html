** How to Play **

You start with 5 baits. Each (bait) question is a chance to catch a fish:

  • Correct answer => you catch a fish.
  • Wrong answer => your bait is wasted.
  • If you get 2 correct answers consecutively, you catch a rare fish and win immediately!
  • If you use all 5 baits without 2 consecutive correct answers, the game ends.
